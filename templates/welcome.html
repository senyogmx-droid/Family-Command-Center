How to retrieve Enphase Gateway details:

Our solar tracking daemon harvests production telemetry directly from your local Envoy or IQ Gateway. Complete setup with your Enphase credentials:
1. Enphase Credentials: Enter your standard login credentials for your Enphase account.
2. Enphase Entrez Portal: Visit the secure Enphase Entrez Portal website.
3. Find Gateway Details: Log in with your credentials. On the portal page:
    • Gateway System Name: Locate the system selection input labeled System. The autocomplete suggestion lists your system name (e.g. Family-Solar-Home).
    • Gateway Serial Number: Select your system, and the Gateway dropdown below will populate with your 12-digit physical gateway serial number (e.g. 122238045678). Copy both values into the fields below.
